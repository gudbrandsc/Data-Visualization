Project goal

The goal of this project is to look at the development of violent crimes that were reported in California(USA), in the years from 2000-2013. By using different visualization methods I hope to uncover different geographic areas that have improved or worsened over time, but also see how the different violent crime types(such as; Robbery or Aggravated assault, etc.) has changed in terms of the number of reports within the dataset time period.
